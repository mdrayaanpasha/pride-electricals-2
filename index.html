Our Services

BESCOM Liason Process

Streamline your BESCOM liaison process with our dedicated services. We handle the complexities of permits, approvals, and compliance, ensuring smooth interactions with BESCOM for uninterrupted electrical services.

Spun Pole Work

Spun pole work encompasses fabricating and installing spun concrete poles for electrical transmission, lighting, and telecommunications. These poles, made by centrifugally casting concrete around a steel core, yield durable, high-strength structures. The process starts with pole design, followed by casting, curing, and finishing.

Internal Wiring Service

Our Internal Wiring Service guarantees seamless electrical connectivity within your premises. Our certified electricians meticulously plan, install, and maintain wiring systems, adhering to safety standards and local regulations. We offer comprehensive inspections, repairs, and upgrades to optimize your electrical infrastructure's efficiency and reliability. Whether residential, commercial, or industrial, trust us for dependable internal wiring solutions tailored to your specific needs.

External Transformer Yard Installation

External transformer yard installation services encompass the planning, construction, and installation of transformer yards outside buildings or facilities. These yards house electrical transformers that adjust voltage for distribution purposes. Services include site evaluation, design, equipment procurement, civil works, electrical installations, and commissioning.
